About

TALAASH is an innovative app that uses AI and face recognition to help find missing persons. Volunteers and community members can easily upload information and images, which are then matched against a database of missing individuals. When a match is found, TALAASH alerts authorities instantly, facilitating faster reunions. With TALAASH, every participant plays a vital role in the search, turning concern into action and helping bring families together.

Community Involvement

How It Works

Our system simplifies the process of locating missing individuals through a user-friendly interface. Volunteers can quickly upload information and images of missing persons, which are then analyzed by our AI algorithms. The face recognition technology compares the uploaded images with those in our database to find potential matches. Upon detecting a match, the system sends instant notifications to the relevant authorities to facilitate a quick response and reunite families.
